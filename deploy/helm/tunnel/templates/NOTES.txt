IMPORTANT - Helm CRD caveat:
  Helm installs CRDs from crds/ on the FIRST install only.
  Helm does NOT upgrade or delete CRDs on subsequent helm upgrade or helm uninstall
  runs. To update CRDs after a chart upgrade, apply them manually:

    kubectl apply --server-side -f \
      https://raw.githubusercontent.com/achetronic/tunnel/main/config/crd/bases/tunnel.achetronic.com_edgenodes.yaml
    kubectl apply --server-side -f \
      https://raw.githubusercontent.com/achetronic/tunnel/main/config/crd/bases/tunnel.achetronic.com_portbindings.yaml

  Or from a local clone of the repository:

    kubectl apply --server-side -f config/crd/bases/
